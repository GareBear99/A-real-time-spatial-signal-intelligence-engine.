## Role in the stack

Where ARC-Core focuses on **entities, events, cases, proposals, and intelligence fusion**, this repository explores the spatial question:

**How do signal observations behave across physical space, time, uncertainty, and structure constraints?**

That makes this repo relevant to:

- RF visualization
- Wi‑Fi mapping
- geospatial telemetry
- propagation experiments
- sensor fusion over space and time
- structure-aware localization research

## Why it matters

This project brings a physical-space layer to the broader ecosystem:

- ARC-Core → correlation and intelligence logic
- Spatial Signal Intelligence Engine → geospatial signal modeling and rendering
- Lucifer runtime / AGI layers → operator-facing execution and future autonomous reasoning over spatial observations
